About Us

Club Description

The Art & Culture Club is a vibrant community for painters, sculptors, photographers, and performers. We host exhibitions, cultural nights, and collaborative projects that showcase Ethiopian heritage alongside contemporary art forms.

Whether you are an experienced artist or picking up a brush for the first time, our open studio sessions welcome everyone.

Club Activities

  • Open studio painting and sculpture sessions
  • Campus art exhibitions twice per semester
  • Cultural heritage showcases and performances
  • Photography walks around Addis Ababa
  • Collaborations with local galleries and artists
  • Art therapy and wellness workshops
