About Us

Club Description

The AAU Hackathon brings together students passionate about innovation, problem-solving, and technology development. Participants collaborate in teams to build creative software and hardware solutions, work on real-world challenges, and present their projects in competitive events. The hackathon encourages teamwork, creativity, and collaboration with mentors to develop impactful ideas.

No prior experience is required — we offer beginner workshops every semester and pair new members with experienced mentors.

Club Activities

  • Skill building workshops and bootcamps
  • Project showcase and Demo Days
  • Mentorship and Industry Collaboration Events
  • National and international competition preparation
  • Guest lectures from industry engineers
  • Collaborative hackathons with other universities
