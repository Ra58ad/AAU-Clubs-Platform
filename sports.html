About Us

Club Description

The AAU Sports Club organizes intramural leagues, inter-faculty tournaments, and training programs in football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics. We promote fitness, teamwork, and school spirit across campus.

All skill levels are welcome. Join a faculty team or sign up for open training sessions held throughout the week.

Club Activities

  • Inter-faculty football and basketball tournaments
  • Weekly training sessions on the main sports field
  • Track and field athletics programs
  • Fitness and conditioning workshops
  • Representation at inter-university competitions
  • Sports day events and charity matches
