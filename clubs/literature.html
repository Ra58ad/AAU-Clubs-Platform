AAU Literature Club

A home for readers, poets, and storytellers who celebrate language, ideas, and Ethiopian literary heritage.

About Us

Who We Are

The AAU Literature Club brings together students who love books, poetry, and creative writing. We host open mic nights, writing workshops, and discussions of classic and contemporary works in Amharic, English, and other languages spoken on campus.

Whether you are drafting your first poem or preparing a manuscript, you will find mentors, peers, and a welcoming audience here.

What We Do

Club Activities

  • Monthly poetry nights and open mic sessions
  • Creative writing workshops with guest authors
  • Book club discussions every two weeks
  • Campus literary magazine editing and publishing
  • Storytelling events celebrating Ethiopian folklore
  • Peer review circles for student writers
