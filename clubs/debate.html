Sharpen your argumentation, research skills, and public speaking in a competitive and supportive environment.

About Us

Who We Are

The AAU Debate Club trains students in British Parliamentary and World Schools debate formats. We compete in national tournaments, host campus debates on current affairs, and mentor new speakers from all faculties.

No prior experience is required — we run beginner clinics every semester and pair new members with experienced debaters.

What We Do

Club Activities

  • Weekly practice debates and argument drills
  • Public speaking and rebuttal workshops
  • Inter-university debate tournaments
  • Mock parliament and policy simulation sessions
  • Research and case-building mentorship
  • Beginner clinics for first-time debaters
