Red Cross AAU Branch

Serving the campus community through humanitarian action, first aid training, and emergency preparedness.

About Us

Who We Are

The Red Cross AAU Branch is a student-led humanitarian organization affiliated with the Ethiopian Red Cross Society. We train volunteers in first aid, coordinate blood donation drives, and respond to campus health and safety needs.

Our mission is to protect life and health, promote human dignity, and build a culture of volunteerism at Addis Ababa University.

What We Do

Club Activities

  • Certified first aid and CPR training workshops
  • Campus blood donation campaigns
  • Disaster preparedness and emergency response drills
  • Community outreach in surrounding neighborhoods
  • Health awareness campaigns on campus
  • Volunteer leadership and humanitarian ethics sessions
